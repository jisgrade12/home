The Big Plan

New and Improved communities

We are going to improve YOUR way to see extra curricular activities at JIS. No more boring things. It's time for a change!

E-sports Tournaments

From FIFA to COD we are here to provide the best E-sports tournament in the history of Alhussan. You will not believe what is about to come...

Sports Tournaments with other schools

What an opportunity! This way not only will you have the fun to play your favorite sports but also there will be a guidance and support of new and un-met people. We don't call them strangers, we just call them yet to be made friends.

Self-sufficient activities

Made to enhance your level of intelligence, we are to host some of the greatest participation opportunities that are not only going to improve your love for academics but also your way to practical life. From Spelling Bees to Math Derbys and from Toastmasters to MUN, we have it all.

A better break experience

This is between me an YOU. We all know the hassle that comes with the break experience provided in the school. Of course it has to be boring but who said it can't be made into another wonder of the world. Well, while we won't be making it so, what we would be doing is enhancing your ticket to a better dine. To serve, we will have from hot and deep chocolate, to soft and chewy cookies.

Better events and facilities

The journey doesn't end itself onto the surface of the canteen, but extends to an even better value of life: events. Not any events though; events that would make your adrenaline rush and your stomach cry. We are planning on brining the joyous moments by brining on Pizza Days, Ice-cream days, Bake sales, Movie Days, and Better trips.
