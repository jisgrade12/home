Description

The students from grade 7 to 10 for the French department had an activity about wellbeing and mental health, in French “le bien-être et la santé mentale”. They wrote their thoughts, express their feeling with drawing or quotes that they believed represent their emotions about this subject. They also wrote what could be the solutions or the good behavior to have during difficult time.
